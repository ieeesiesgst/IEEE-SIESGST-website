MTT-S CHAPTER

The Microwave theory and techniques society was inaugurated in the year 2019 and promotes the advancement of microwave theory and its applications, including RF, microwave, millimeter-wave, and terahertz technologies.

Our Achievements

Visit to SAMEER 2020

Our MTT-S members got the opportunity to visit the Society of Applied Microwave and Electronic Engineering Research (SAMEER) in Kharghar, Navi Mumbai where they were showcased the various tests conducted by them before any product enters the market. The research centre focuses on the safety of products after they enter the market for radiation, durability during daily use and environmental safety.

RF KIT

IEEE MTT-S was gifted a RF kit for free which was worth $500. That kit was used for workshops conducted for students.

IMaRC 2019

Members of IEEE MTT-S SIES GST had attended IMaR conference 2019. For attending this conference, our two members got scholarship.

Jio Iv

An Industrial visit was organized in partnership with Jio, Navi Mumbai. Future technologies by Jio were showcased.

Our Goal
